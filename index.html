My Favourite New Zealand Locations

Queenstown. Most popular tourist destination in NZ for good reason. Beautiful mountain scenery.

Lake Tekapo. Known for picturesque stargazing views. Image shows the famous church built there.

Cape Reinga. Very top of NZ, you can see where the Pacific Ocean & Tasman Sea meet.

Rotorua. Maori Cultural capital of NZ. Strong sulfuric smell (smells like eggs) but you get used to it.
